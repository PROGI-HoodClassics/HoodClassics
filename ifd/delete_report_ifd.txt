==== DELETE STORY ====
Form:
	POST /api/story/delete/{story_id}

Responses:
	- success - 200 OK "Story deleted"
	- not a moderator or author - 401 UNAUTHORIZED "Not a moderator or the author"
	- no story in database - 404 NOT FOUND "Story doesn't exist"
